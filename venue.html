SUNY Global Center

ACNS 2026 will be held in the Global Classroom at the SUNY Global Center in midtown Manhattan. Housed in a distinguished landmark building at 116 East 55th Street, the SUNY Global Center is the headquarters for the State University of New York in New York City. Built in 1927, the five story, Georgian style mansion was the Ziegler residence, designated a historic landmark by the New York City Landmark Preservation Commission in 2001.
